Episode 3: Fake news, misinformation and scams in the dark web

27 June, 2020

About this Episode

In this episode of DEFCON Trivandrum Malayalam podcast, we are having Nandakishore Harikumar as a guest. The host, Abhijith and Nandakishore discussing the misinformation and fake news spreading on the internet and guest reveal the truth and real stories that happened in recent times due to the spreading of fake news and misinformation. Later Nandakishore explains about scams and other illegal activities happening in the dark web, how the cyber criminals take advantage of dark web etc. Kishore also shares an interesting story he experienced during a dark web monitoring activity.

About the Guest

Nandakishore Harikumar

Nandakishore Harikumar is the CEO and Founder of a Cyber Security Startup named Technisanct. He is an Engineer turned Entrepreneur. His startup is backed by IIT Mandi, Data Security Council of India (DCSI), India Accelarator Program and GAN(Global Accelarator Network). He was also the co-founder of the private intelligence firm Seclabs and Systems based out in Noida, Delhi
